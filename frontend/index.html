Accuracy limitations

This detector is trained on older TTS systems and their spectrogram patterns. It achieves high accuracy on training-era synthetic speech but only about 60% accuracy on modern TTS (e.g. ElevenLabs). Do not rely on it for definitive authenticity of newer synthetic voices.
